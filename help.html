What's the point?

The Most Dangerous Writing App is designed to shut down your inner editor and get you into a state of flow. If you stop typing for more than five seconds, all progress will be lost.

Because 'tis better to have written and lost, than never to have written at all.

What's the deal with #NaNoWriMo?

The National Novel Writing Month is an annual challange for writers to write the first draft of a novel from scratch to finish in November.

If you want to support your NaNoWriMo friends and MDWA, please spread the love on twitter:

I used the Most Dangerous Writing App for my #NaNoWriMo novel today!

Since I believe in eating my own dog food, I am writing an entire novel using this app; one thousand six hundred and sixty-seven words a day in a single run. It is the fourth scariest thing I ever did.
